Family & Kids Options

Coal Harbour Bike Rental is very family-friendly. We have several ways to get kids rolling safely along the seawall.

  • Kids’ bikes: separate small bikes in 16", 20", and 24" sizes.
  • Child seats: front seat for children up to about 1 year old; rear seat designed for kids up to about 5 years old.
  • Trail-along extension: attaches to an adult bike so kids can pedal while you steer.
  • Bike trailer: enclosed trailer that can carry two small children.
  • Tandem bikes: perfect for couples or a parent riding with an older child.

Electric choices

For riders aged 18 and over, we offer a range of electric options for effortless climbs and longer adventures.

  • E-bikes: available with both pedal-assist and throttle systems, so you can choose how much you want to pedal.
  • E-scooters: nimble and easy to ride, ideal for exploring the seawall and nearby neighbourhoods.
  • Helmets and locks are included with electric rentals.

Repairs & maintenance

In addition to rentals, we also do bike repair and maintenance. From flat fixes to tune-ups, our mechanics keep both our fleet and your personal bike running smoothly.

  • Flat tire repair and tube replacement
  • Brake and gear adjustments
  • Chain cleaning and lubrication
  • Safety checks before big rides
