About Coal Harbour Bike Rental

Coal Harbour Bike Rental is a local, family-friendly shop founded in 2019 just steps away from Vancouver’s famous seawall. We specialise in making it easy and comfortable for everyone—from first-time riders to seasoned cyclists—to explore Stanley Park and the waterfront on two wheels.

Our team is known for warm, friendly service and clear advice. Whether you’re visiting Vancouver for the first time or planning a regular weekend ride, we’ll help you choose the right bike, explain the route, and make sure you feel confident before you roll out.

Where we started

Coal Harbour Bike Rental opened its doors in 2019 with a simple idea: give people an easy way to experience Vancouver’s seawall and city views without worrying about equipment or planning.

We’re a small, owner-operated shop located at 1570 Coal Harbour Quay, Vancouver, BC V6G 3E9, a short ride from the entrance to Stanley Park. Every bike and scooter is tuned daily so your ride feels smooth, safe, and fun.

Over the years, we’ve built a reputation for being approachable and family-friendly. Many of our customers are repeat visitors who come back because they appreciate the helpful route suggestions, honest advice, and relaxed atmosphere in the shop.

  • Friendly, down-to-earth staff who love talking about routes.
  • Kids & family-friendly options, including child seats and trailers.
  • Quick check-in process so you can start your ride within minutes.

Family & Kids-Friendly Rentals

One of the reasons Coal Harbour Bike Rental is so popular is how easy we make it for families to ride together. We have a wide range of options designed with kids in mind.

  • Kids’ bikes: separate small bikes in 16", 20", and 24" sizes.
  • Child seats: front seats suitable for very young riders (around 1 year old) and rear seats for kids up to about 5 years.
  • Trail-along extensions: attachments that connect to an adult bike so kids can pedal while you steer.
  • Bike trailers: enclosed trailers that can comfortably carry two small children.
  • Tandem bikes: a fun option for couples or a parent riding with an older child.

Our staff are patient and happy to help you adjust helmets, set up seats, and choose a route that feels safe and enjoyable for younger riders.

Why riders choose Coal Harbour

Riders often describe Coal Harbour Bike Rental as a relaxed, reliable starting point for exploring Vancouver. The shop is praised for its clean, well-maintained bikes, clear explanations, and the way staff take time to answer questions before you head out.

With a mix of traditional bikes, road bikes for athletes, kids’ bikes, tandems, and electric options like e-bikes and e-scooters, there’s something for every type of rider. Combined with competitive pricing and a location right by the water, it’s an easy choice for both locals and visitors who want a smooth and memorable ride.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Coal Harbour Bike Rental offer?

Coal Harbour Bike Rental provides a variety of bike and scooter rentals, including kids’ bikes, single bikes, tandem bikes, road bikes, and electric options such as e-bikes and e-scooters for exploring the area around Stanley Park.

What is the location of the venue?

The shop is located at 1570 Coal Harbour Quay, Vancouver, BC V6G 3E9, just steps from the seawall and Stanley Park.

Are there any additional fees for equipment like locks or baskets?

Some accessories, such as locks or baskets, may involve a small extra fee. It’s best to ask at the counter when you choose your rental so we can explain all options clearly.

Is customer service available if I encounter issues with my rental?

Yes. Our staff are available throughout your rental period to help with questions or minor bike issues so you can continue your ride with confidence.

How long does it typically take to rent a bike?

In most cases, the process takes just a few minutes once you choose your bike and size. We’ll adjust the seat, provide a map if needed, and you’re ready to go.
